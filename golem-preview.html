Amber Golem — Preview

Four states shown at 3× scale so you can see the sprite. From left: idle, chasing, windup (slam telegraph ring), after slam (shards flying).

IdleChaseWindupSlam!

Behavior: 7 HP (vs 3 for a wolf). Speed 0.55 (less than half a wolf's). Detection range 230px. Attack range 48px. Windup is 38 frames (~0.6s) — the dashed ring gives you time to dodge. Slam does 2 damage with knockback (tradeoff for being slow), plus shards pop out. During the boss fight, any golem inside the arena is teleported safely out.
